What We Offer

Everything you need to manage
inventory with confidence.

Batch-Level Tracking

Add products with batch numbers, quantities, and expiry dates. Know exactly where every unit is, at any time.

Expiry Alerts

Get notified 30 days before stock expires. Act before it's too late — avoid wastage and compliance issues.

FIFO Auto-Selling

When you sell, INVENTRACK automatically deducts from the earliest-expiring batch first. No manual effort.

Real-Time Dashboard

A clean overview of all your stock, expiry timelines, and alerts — updated instantly every time you make a move.

Secure User Accounts

Each business gets its own isolated inventory. Your data is never shared or visible to other users.

Mobile-Friendly

Fully responsive design — manage stock from your phone on the warehouse floor or your desktop in the office.
