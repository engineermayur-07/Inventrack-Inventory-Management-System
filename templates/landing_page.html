What We Offer

Everything you need to manage
inventory with confidence.

Purpose-built tools that remove guesswork from inventory management and keep you always in control.

📦

Batch-Level Tracking

Add products with batch numbers, quantities, and expiry dates. Know exactly where every unit is, at any time, across all batches.

⏰

Expiry Alert System

Get 30-day advance warnings before any batch expires. Act before it's too late — avoid wastage and compliance issues.

⚡

FIFO Auto-Selling

When you sell, INVENTRACK automatically deducts from the earliest-expiring batch first. No manual effort, no errors.

⊞

Real-Time Dashboard

A clean, live overview of all your stock, expiry timelines, and key metrics — updated instantly every time you act.

🔐

Isolated User Accounts

Each business gets its own private inventory space. Your data is never shared with or visible to other accounts.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Fully responsive — manage stock from your phone on the warehouse floor or your desktop in the office. Seamlessly.
