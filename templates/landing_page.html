Why INVENTRACK

Everything your inventory needs

Built for speed, accuracy, and expiry awareness — from batch entry to final sale.

Batch-Level Tracking
Every stock entry is tied to a unique batch number, so you always know exactly where each unit came from and when it expires.
Automatic FIFO Selling
Powered by a Min-Heap, sales always pull from the nearest-to-expiry batch first — no manual sorting, no wasted stock.
Expiry Alerts
Color-coded warnings at 30 days, 10 days, and day-of-expiry keep you ahead of losses before they happen.
Live Dashboard
Instant overview of all your products, batches, quantities, and expiry statuses — filterable and searchable in real time.
Per-User Isolation
Each account manages its own private inventory. No data bleeds between users — session-backed and secure by design.
Responsive by Default
Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile with a collapsible sidebar and a touch-friendly interface throughout.
Workflow

Simple to use, smart inside

Three steps to a fully managed, expiry-aware inventory.

1
Add a Batch
Enter the product name, batch number, quantity, and expiry date. The system loads it into memory instantly.
2
Monitor Expiry
Your dashboard highlights batches approaching expiry in real time — no guesswork, no spreadsheets.
3
Sell Products
Enter a product and quantity. INVENTRCK automatically fulfills the order from the oldest batches first, across multiple batches if needed.
