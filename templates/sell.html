FIFO Dispatch Logic
  • 1
    Product lookup All batches matching the product name are retrieved from your inventory.
  • 2
    Earliest expiry first The batch closest to its expiry date is selected and units are deducted from it first.
  • 3
    Cascade if needed If a single batch doesn't cover the quantity, the next oldest batch is used automatically.
  • 4
    Empty batches removed Any batch reduced to zero units is deleted from the inventory automatically.

If total available stock across all batches is less than the requested quantity, the sale will be rejected and nothing will be deducted.
